Thank you for downloading this asset.

This package provide the vertex and fragment pencil sketch shader for your game.

The PencilSketchStyleShaderDemo.unity demonstrate the pencil sketch shader effect.

Shader settings :

_MainColor - the main color of pencil sketch
_OutlineColor - the color of outline
_OutlineWidth - the width of outline
_PencilSketchDensity - the line density of pencil sketch
_PencilSketchTexture0 - pencil sketch sampling texture 0 
_PencilSketchTexture1 - pencil sketch sampling texture 1 
_PencilSketchTexture2 - pencil sketch sampling texture 2 
_PencilSketchTexture3 - pencil sketch sampling texture 3
_PencilSketchTexture4 - pencil sketch sampling texture 4
_PencilSketchTexture5 - pencil sketch sampling texture 5
